ABOUT US

Marmoka came together by chance when each member crossed paths through shared connections in the electronic music scene. What started as a few jam sessions soon turned into a full-fledged band, and the rest is history.

Karmele is responsible for team management, agenda, and social media. This girl from Bilbao doesn’t produce music, but she’s aware of current trends.

Mikel takes care of live music and synthesizers. He’s from Bilbao and is very interested in progressive electronics. He’s inspired by underground music and Avicii.

Ekain creates electronic percussion and rhythmic bases. He’s from Iparralde, specifically from Ainhoa. He likes to mix electronic music from Norway with tech-house.

Leire is the singer and cares about creating visual content. She follows the vocalists of Icelandic music. Sensitive and shy, but intense on stage.

Alex, born in Donosti, is a DJ. He likes ambient electronic music with Nordic touches. Inspired by Aoki and unknown Icelandic musicians.
